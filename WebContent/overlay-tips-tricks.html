Help, Tips, and Tricks

About User Preferences
Most information regarding user preferences on the Exceptions Approvals Tool is saved locally for each user, on their web browser.
These preferences can range from the exceptions generated/submitted submitted, to filters applied, to fields displayed/hidden, etc...
To guarantee performance and convenience, none of this information is sent to the tool server, with the exception of the 'Saved Views'.
If for some reason the user erases their cookies and history, all of his/her preferences will be lost.
